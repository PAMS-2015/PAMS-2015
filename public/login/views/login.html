PAMS

Product Authentication

PAMS determines the authenticity of brand name products thereby ensuring the protection of brands, retailers and consumers alike.

How does PAMS work?

It's simple:

  1. Find a product
  2. Find the serial number
  3. Verify authenticity
  4. Repeat

When PAMS detects a counterfeit product, that product information is recorded, including exactly who entered it along with when & where it was entered.

This information is saved and integrated with Google Maps in order to detect trends in the market.
